Privacy Policy: NHL Playoff Tracker

Effective date: April 22, 2026

1. No data collection

The NHL Playoff Tracker extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information, browsing history, or user data.

2. Data usage

The extension only communicates with api-web.nhle.com to retrieve standings, club schedules, scoreboard data, and related information shown in the popup. No user-identifying information is sent during these requests.

3. Third-party links

When you click on game highlights (recap, condensed, or GameCenter), you are directed to nhl.com. Those visits are governed by the NHL’s privacy policy.

4. Changes to this policy

Any updates to this policy will be posted here.
